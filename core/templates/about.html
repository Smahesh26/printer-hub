Who We Are

Printer Hub is an online marketplace dedicated exclusively to printers. Whether you're a small business owner looking to buy a reliable laser printer, a student who needs to rent one for a project, or a seller wanting to reach thousands of buyers — Printer Hub is the right place for you.

Founded in 2024, we operate out of Bangalore, India, and serve customers across the country. Our platform cuts out the middleman, giving buyers better prices and sellers more reach.
